Supporting Our Community

Arts Partner: Judy Andrucki

A civic minded and savvy arts supporter for many years in Lewiston/Auburn, L/A Arts is delighted to give our first Arts Partner award to Judith Andrucki. With her work as a board member of Maine Music Society and supporter of multiple local and cultural institutions with her time, energy and business skills, Judy has established herself as a steward for arts and culture in the Twin Cities. Her support for L/A Arts has been unwavering, giving her expertise and insightful advice to the organization on best practices across multiple aspects of the business. Her generosity, dedication, and belief in L/A Arts has been instrumental to our success over the years, and we are truly grateful for her support and service to us.
